SUNSHINE RESORT CUSTOMER ANALYSIS

  • Check it out!

Sunshine Resort, a luxurious beachfront hotel management company, sought to enhance guest experiences, optimize hotel operations, and maximize revenue by gaining deeper insights into their reservation data. As a data analyst, I performed a comprehensive analysis of customer demographics, booking patterns, and churn rate using Microsoft Excel pivot tables. Through targeted strategies, I successfully reduced the churn rate to 10%, indicating that 90% of guests return or complete their bookings. This improvement in customer retention led to increased revenue, customer loyalty, and positive word-of-mouth, ultimately enhancing the resort's reputation and competitiveness in the hospitality industry.

    KEY FINDINGS

      1. Customer Database: The hotel resort database contains 100,000+ customers.
      2. Average Length of Stay: The average length of stay for guests is 3.5 days for weekend nights and 2.8 days for weekday nights.
      3. Country Distribution: The majority of guests come from Portugal (40%), followed by the United Kingdom (25%) and the United States (15%).
      4. Agent Bookings: 30% of customers come through agents.
      5. Peak Booking Months: August and July experience the highest number of bookings.
      6. Room Type Reservations: The most frequently reserved room types are Deluxe Ocean View and Superior Garden View.
      7. Booking Changes: Guests make an average of 1.5 changes to their bookings, with room type changes being the most common
      8. Change Frequency and Stay Length: Guests who make more changes tend to have shorter stays.
      9. Deposit Types: The preferred type of deposit is credit card (80%).
      10. Repeated Guests: 20% of visitors are repeated guests.
      11. Hotel and Customer Type Analysis: Filtering the dashboard by hotel column and/or customer type reveals valuable insights into booking patterns and preferences.

    INSIGHTS AND RECOMMENDATIONS

      - Target marketing efforts towards Portuguese and UK customers.
      - Optimize room allocation and pricing strategies during peak booking months.
      - Offer personalized services to repeated guests.
      - Streamline booking changes and room type assignments.
      - Enhance customer retention strategies to reduce churn rate.
